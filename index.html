Tips & Tricks

Hair Tips

Tip 1

Keep Your Hair Hydrated

Healthy hair starts with hydration! No matter your hair type, using a deep-conditioning treatment at least once a week can keep your locks shiny and smooth. Ask your stylist about the best products for your hair’s needs, especially if you color, braid, or relax your hair regularly.

Tip 2

Less Heat, More Care

Overusing heat tools can cause damage and split ends. Try to limit the use of flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. Instead, opt for air-drying when possible, or ask our experts about heat-protectant products that help you style without the damage.

Tip 1

Regular trims = Stronger hair

Frequent trims might sound counterintuitive when you’re growing your hair, but they actually help! Removing split ends every 6-8 weeks keeps your hair healthy, prevents breakage, and promotes faster, stronger growth.
